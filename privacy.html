Privacy and Cookie statement

What personal information do we collect and how do we use cookies on our website.

Personal information

What personal information does masterstake.net collect?
We collect the following personal information:

- Email address and IP address when an order is created.
- IP address and browser information when a user visits our website.

Does masterstake.net share personal information?
We do not share personal information.


Cookies

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are used to store settings for a website.
Your browser downloads these small text files and saves them on your harddrive.

Our website uses the following cookies:

- A cookie to store the user preference for our learning center.
- Analytical cookies to recognise and to count number of visitors to our website.
