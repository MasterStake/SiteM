What we do

We make blockchain technology available for everyone.
Create your own coin with our coin wizard without any knowledge of programming.

Get started

We offer you a functional coin, based a recent source code of Bitcoin, Litecoin, Blackcoin, Dash and PIVX. The coin is delivered as a software download with a Windows and Linux wallet.

Additional products and services are available trough “MyCoin”, the self-service portal for your coin.
